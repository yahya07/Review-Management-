How It Works

A simple, intelligent process that protects and enhances your reputation

1

Generate Your Unique QR Code

Get a custom QR code with your business logo and name. Place it on tables, receipts, or anywhere customers can easily scan.

2

Customer Rates Their Experience

Customers scan the code and see a simple 5-star rating page. Quick, easy, and mobile-friendly.

3

Smart Routing Based on Rating

3 stars or less: Customers are directed to a private feedback form with a discount code to make things right.

4-5 stars: Happy customers are encouraged to share their experience on Google Maps with an additional reward.

4

Manage Everything from Your Dashboard

Track feedback, analyze trends, adjust offers, and watch your reputation grow—all from one powerful dashboard.
